Systems Operation

Kuelewa undani wa utendaji wa mifumo ya mitambo

Hydraulic Main System
Mfumo wa hydraulic unatumia kanuni ya Negative Flow Control (NFC). Wakati lever ziko katikati (Neutral), pampu inatoa mtiririko mdogo wa mafuta ili kupunguza matumizi ya mafuta. Lever ikisogezwa, shinikizo la NFC linapungua, na kusababisha Swashplate ya pampu kutanuka na kuongeza mtiririko.
MTIRIRIKO WA MAFUTA (OIL FLOW):
1
Tanki la Hydraulic: Mafuta yanatolewa na kuelekea kwenye pampu kuu (Main Pump).
2
Main Pump: Inageuza nguvu ya mekanika kuwa nguvu ya hydraulic (Pressure).
3
Control Valve: Inagawa mafuta kuelekea kwenye silinda (Boom, Bucket, Stick).
  • Main Relief Pressure 35,000 kPa (5075 psi)
  • Pilot Pressure 4,100 kPa (595 psi)
  • Pump Type Variable Displacement Piston Pump
Engine Air & Exhaust
Mfumo wa hewa ni wa aina ya Turbocharged Aftercooled. Hewa inaingizwa kupitia Turbo, inashindiliwa (compressed), na kisha inapozwa na Aftercooler ili kuongeza msongamano wa oksijeni kabla ya kuingia kwenye chumba cha mlipuko (Cylinder).
PHEASE ZA UTENDAJI:

1. Intake: Turbo inapokea hewa safi na kuisukuma kwa nguvu.
2. Compression: Piston inapanda juu na kushindilia hewa na mafuta.
3. Power: Mlipuko unatokea (Combustion) na kuisukuma piston chini.
4. Exhaust: Gesi iliyotumika inatoka na kuzungusha turbocharger turbine.

Turbo Boost 18-24 PSI
Exhaust Temp 450°C - 600°C
Fuel System (Common Rail)

Kwenye mifumo ya HEUI au Common Rail, mafuta yanashindiliwa na High-Pressure Pump kisha yanakaa kwenye bomba la pamoja (Rail). ECM inadhibiti Solenoid Injectors kufungua kwa muda maalum (Micro-seconds) kulingana na mahitaji ya kasi ya injini.

  • Rail Pressure 120 - 180 MPa
  • Injector Voltage 70V - 105V Peak
  • Fuel Quality Sensor Inductive / Capacitive Type
KIDOKEZO CHA FUNDI

Ukiona moshi mweusi, mara nyingi ni hitilafu ya mtiririko wa hewa (Air flow) au turbo kushindwa kutoa presha ya kutosha.
